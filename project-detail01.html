+ Project Descriptions #01

Introducing 545wyn, a ground-up new construction 298,000 square foot creative office and exclusive retail building located at 545 NW 26th Street in the dynamic Wynwood neighborhood of Miami.

  • Region : Miami, Florida
  • Completed Date : January 2021
  • Architectural use : office building
  • Working day : 225
  • Member : 320
  • Budget : 3M

Office, artfully done.

As the first Class A office building in Miami’s burgeoning Wynwood neighborhood, 545wyn engages and extends the energy of the community while attracting tenants that fit best with the district and the property.

Three purposeful goals

Developed by Sterling Bay and designed by Gensler, 545wyn was created with three purposeful goals: to elevate and cultivate Miami’s standard office experience, authentically reproduce Wynwood’s vibrancy, and engage with the Wynwood neighborhood.

545wyn’s unique site shape

545wyn’s unique site shape also provided an opportunity for the team to design a structure respectful to the scale and character of the neighborhood by creating efficient floor plates and unique outdoor spaces that appeal to the tech-focused tenants, and the community at large.

The real market differentiator is a new kind of office that allows tenants to sit, stand, and work while moving through sheltered, tempered outdoor space — all while feeling healthier and more connected to their community.
